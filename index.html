About us

Ejaridini was founded in 2022 by Innocentia Khanyisile Fakude. Initially, she was a street vendor with her menu exclusively featuring sandwiches. She has since worked on expanding her menu and building a physical location for her business and officially launched Ejaridini Kitchen on December 16th of 2025 in Standerton extension 4. Ejaridini Kitchen has since gained popularity among locals for their unique ability to unite communities, families, and strengthening bonds between friends through good food. They are especially popular for their pleasant customer service, endless gratitude, and consistent communication with their valued customers.

Ejaridini Kitchen has played a role in combating youth unemployment in Standerton by employing 4 local residents since opening its doors. We are committed to uniting communities through good food and uplifting them by creating opportunities for growth.
