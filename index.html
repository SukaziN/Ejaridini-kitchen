About us

Ejaridini was founded in 2022 by Nomfundo Cindy Mtolo. Initially, she was a street vendor with her menu exclusively featuring sandwiches. She has since worked on expanding her menu and building a physical location for her business and officially launched Ejaridini Kitchen on December 16th of 2025 in Standerton extension 4. Ejaridini Kitchen has since gained popularity among locals for their unique ability to unite communities, families, and strengthening bonds between friends through good food. They are especially popular for their pleasant customer service, endless gratitude, and consistent communication with their valued customers.
